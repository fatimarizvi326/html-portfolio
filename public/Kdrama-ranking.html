My Top 4 Kdrama of all-time

The King Eternal Monarch

This drama is about two parallel worlds and time travel. I like this drama because it has alot og genre like romance, sci-fi, action, mystery, drama and crime. The direction and cinematography of this drama is very well done. Alot of people find it difficult to understand and get confused because of different worlds and time travel. But it truely is one of my favourite Kdrama. It is very well made.

Alchemy of Souls

This drama is a fantasy based. Again it is best for alot of reasons, it has one of the best cinematography effect. The story is very well written. It is a package of action, magic, mystery, romance and comedy.

What's wrong with secretary kim

This drama is a romcom. It's very refresh, both the leads have very good chemistry. It has balance of romance, comedy. The male lead has sassy character and he portray it very well.

Business Proposal

This drama is also my one of favourite. It is a romcom. Both the male and female leads have their owm uniqueness and have great chemistry. Even the second lead of this drama is so good and one of my favourite second lead.
